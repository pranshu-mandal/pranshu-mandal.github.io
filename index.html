About Me

I am an astrophysicist with a Ph.D. from the University of Tsukuba, with expertise spanning multiple disciplines unified by developing innovative technical solutions to extract meaningful information from complex data. My research bridges astronomy, signal processing, machine learning, and medical applications, with a focus on democratizing access to scientific tools and knowledge.

My work demonstrates capacity for independent research, interdisciplinary collaboration, and scientific innovation. From developing data analysis pipelines for radio astronomy to creating AI systems for healthcare applications, I specialize in translating complex technical challenges into practical solutions.

Currently, my research focus has shifted toward AI applications in signal processing, medical implementation, and computer vision, building on my foundation in astronomical instrumentation and data analysis.

Research

Research Interests

  • AI Applications in Healthcare: Deep learning for predictive seizure detection and EEG analysis
  • Signal Processing & Computer Vision: Advanced algorithms for complex data analysis
  • Radio Astronomy Instrumentation: MKID camera development and data processing pipelines
  • Scientific Democratization: Low-cost instrumentation and accessible research tools

Current Research Focus

My research currently prioritizes AI applications in signal processing, medical implementation, and computer vision. Building on my foundation in astronomical data analysis, I have developed predictive AI models for seizure detection achieving 92% sensitivity with 15-minute advance warning. I also created the ChunkedPCA algorithm for noise reduction, improving signal-to-noise ratios by up to 15% in challenging observational conditions.

Key Innovations

I specialize in creating end-to-end technical solutions: from comprehensive MKID data analysis pipelines at Nobeyama Observatory to real-time AI systems for clinical EEG monitoring. My work emphasizes both scientific rigor and practical implementation, with systems designed for real-world deployment and accessibility.
