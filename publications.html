Detection of pre-ictal epileptic events using a self-attention based neural network from raw Neonatal EEG data

Authors: Kondaveeti Tejaswi, Madala Vikas, Himala Praharsha, Pranshu Mandal, Sujan Chakraborty, Olaf Wolkenhauer, Saptarshi Bej*

Journal: Computers in Biology and Medicine, 2025

DOI: 10.1016/j.compbiomed.2025.110518

Abstract: This interdisciplinary research applies advanced neural network techniques with self-attention mechanisms to detect pre-ictal epileptic events in neonatal EEG data, contributing to early detection and treatment of epilepsy in newborns.
